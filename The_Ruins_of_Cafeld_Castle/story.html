Quick Campaign

The Ruins of Cafeld Castle

A forgotten ruin deep in the misty woods. Exploration, mystery, combat, and puzzles, with flexible approaches for level 1 adventurers.

Level 1 Exploration Puzzles Mystery

Synopsis

An elven scholar named Derga hires the party to explore long-lost Cafeld Castle, an overgrown ruin rumored to be haunted by strange lights and eerie voices. Within lie powerful artifacts and lost knowledge—if the adventurers can survive. Will they uncover the secrets of Cafeld, or become its next victims?

Act 1: The Offer

Meeting at the Green Owl Tavern

  • Cafeld Castle was abandoned centuries ago after its last ruler vanished under mysterious circumstances.
  • Legends say the ruins are haunted, but Derga believes they hide something of great historical value.
  • She needs help with dangers inside—wild creatures, bandits, or worse.

Reward: 50 gp each and keep any valuables found (except key historical relics).

DC 12 Insight reveals Derga is excited but nervous—she may know more than she's saying.

Derga, Elven Scholar (Non‑Combatant Ally)

Medium Elf, NG. Slender, silver‑haired, emerald eyes; deep green robes with golden runes; satchel of books; silver circlet; quiet confidence.

  • AC 12 (robes), HP 9 (2d8), Speed 30 ft.
  • Abilities STR 10, DEX 12, CON 10, INT 16, WIS 14, CHA 12
  • Saves INT +5, WIS +4; Skills Arcana +5, History +5, Insight +4
  • Senses Darkvision 60 ft.; Languages Common, Elvish, Draconic

Scholarly Guidance. Once per short rest, grant +1d4 to an INT or WIS check while researching or solving a puzzle.

Tactics: Avoids combat; offers lore and puzzle hints.

Act 2: The Journey to Cafeld

A half‑day through tangled forest paths.

  • Collapsed Bridge. Athletics DC 10 to climb down/up or solve creatively.
    Stone teeth of a once-proud span jut from the chasm like broken fangs. Mist swirls below; damp moss slicks the remaining stones. A rope frayed to white threads dangles on the far side, swaying to a rhythm you can't feel.
  • Signs of Others. Bandit tracks suggest rivals are ahead. (Do the DC check here.)
    Boot-prints trample damp leaf litter; charred birch twigs mark where someone doused a fire in haste. A blue-dyed scrap of cloth (Sarka's sash color) snags on a thorn, fluttering like a warning pennant.
  • Restless Spirit. Pale figure watches from trees, vanishes when approached.
    Between the boles of ancient oaks, moon-pale eyes hover in the gloom. The air grows ten degrees colder; breath pearls in front of lips even at midday. Footfalls crunch where no one stands.
  • Shortcut. Survival or Perception DC 13 spots a secret trail and saves time
    A narrow seam in the undergrowth—vines stitched over bent hazel wands—hides the hunter's path. Pressing aside the dew-beaded ivy releases the scents of crushed fern and wet loam. Faint chalk sigils of an owl mark stones at ankle height every twenty paces, pointing the way.

Act 3: The Ruins of Cafeld Castle

A crumbling fortress, entrance choked by fallen stones and creeping vines. Above a worn archway, faded Elvish reads: "Only those who seek wisdom shall pass unharmed."

Black ivy grips the outer walls like ink-stained fingers. In the courtyard, a dry fountain holds rainwater the color of tarnished silver; pale lilies float where coins once glimmered. Crows watch from a shattered belfry, their silhouettes like ink blots against a wan sky.

Investigation or History DC 12: home to a secretive elven order researching powerful magic before they disappeared.

Hall of Echoes

Mirrors—tall as sentries—wear veils of dust, yet each reflection gleams too sharply, lagging a half‑breath behind your movements. A dropped whisper returns as someone else's voice from the far end of the hall.

Puzzle Clue: The phrase "Light reveals the past, shadow conceals the truth." is etched faintly above the entrance. Speaking it deactivates the mirrors and reveals a hidden passage.

Failure: Shadowy figures emerge to attack (2 Shadows, adjusted for level 1).

Bandit Camp

A ring of soot-black stones hems a dead fire. Bedrolls reek of iron and wet leather. Dice—one corner blood‑specked—sit mid‑game. From saplings overhead dangle tin charms that clink in the breeze like quiet alarms.

Heart of Cafeld

The pedestal rises from a mosaic of owls in flight—wings outstretched toward the cardinal points. The orb's light pulses with a heartbeat cadence, casting long ribs of radiance across carved bookshelves drowned in shadow.

The Tests

Test of Knowledge

Riddle: "I have no mouth, yet I tell the truth. I move without legs, yet I guide the way. I am not alive, yet I grow and change. What am I?"

Answer: A book (or written word / knowledge / history).

Test of Courage

Minor combat against a magical guardian.

Test of Wisdom

Three statues: eyes covered, ears covered, mouth covered.

Riddle: "One speaks only lies. One sees truth with blind eyes. One listens yet knows nothing. Which holds true wisdom?"

Answer: The one covering their mouth — silence, patience, reflection.

Act 4: The Aftermath

  • Returning to Hearthmere, Derga studies the orb and pays the party.
  • Hints of deeper secrets and future expeditions into Cafeld.
  • If the party keeps the orb, Derga warns they may have unleashed a terrible power.

Potential Twists & Future Hooks

  • The orb imprisons an ancient entity.
  • A larger faction seeks the ruin's power.
  • Derga belongs to a secretive elven order researching lost magic.
