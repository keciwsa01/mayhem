üåô An Unexpected Encounter

"If you find a pouch of black seeds, I'll pay you five times what the old man offers. Just leave them for me outside the orchard fence, wrapped in this cloth."

üé≠ Roleplay Notes

Tyndareus's Secret

The guard's journal reveals young "Tyn" was romantically involved with Grammy's staff. This adds emotional weight to his quest - it's not just about the pie, but about recapturing lost youth and love.

Goblin Personalities

  • Bakery Goblins: Pie-obsessed, curious, not malicious
  • Ruckwort: Proud "chef," wears berry crown, protective of "his" kitchen
  • Patrol Goblin: Oblivious, humming, juggling apples

The Mystery Seeds

Grammy's warning suggests these seeds create corrupted plants. The mysterious woman's interest hints at darker purposes. Perfect hook for future adventures!

üî• "We Burn Everything Down!"

  • Mac the Treant awakens and becomes hostile if fire threatens the orchard
  • Dryads cast Entangle and flee, calling for nature's revenge
  • Fire spreads: DC 15 Athletics/Acrobatics every round or take 1d6 fire damage
  • Recipe burns: Tyndareus is heartbroken but still pays half reward for trying
  • Consequence: Local druids and rangers become hostile to the party

‚öîÔ∏è "Murder All the Goblins!"

  • Ruckwort flees and returns with 2d4 goblin reinforcements
  • Oven elementals become agitated by violence, all ovens activate
  • Bakery catches fire from elemental chaos (see fire rules above)
  • Recipe survives if players are quick, but building becomes unstable
  • Moral consequence: Tyndareus is disappointed in their methods

üí∞ "We Keep Everything!"

  • Tyndareus casts Zone of Truth when they return
  • If they lie: His imp follows them invisibly and reports back
  • If they're honest: He's sad but understanding, pays 25 gp for their honesty
  • Mysterious woman still appears and offers her deal
  • Future consequence: Tyndareus won't hire them again

üå± "We Plant the Evil Seeds!"

  • 24 hours later: Twisted apple tree sprouts (use Awakened Tree stats)
  • Tree attacks: Anyone who approaches, spreads corrupted fruit
  • Environmental: 100-foot radius becomes difficult terrain
  • Solution: Must be burned or blessed to stop spreading
  • Hook: Perfect setup for a follow-up adventure!

üèÉ "We Just Leave!"

  • Pays 10 gp for their time and honesty
  • Mysterious woman approaches them later with a different job
  • Goblins eventually overrun the nearby town
  • Future hook: Town guard hires them to deal with goblin problem
